# Daily Routine
## Water
* 2 to 3 Litres daily
## Breakfast
### Frozen Fruit Milkshake
* A2 Milk
* Frozen Fruit
	* Mango
	* Banana
		* Let bananas ripen fully
		* Peel and freeze
	* Berries
## Lunch
### Salmon Sandwiches
* 4x slices of Wholemeal Bread
* 95g can of Flavoured Salmon (divided evenly between two sandwiches)
* One portion of Frozen Spinach (divided evenly between two sandwiches)
	* Microwave on defrost for 2-3 minutes
	* Use a dedicated tea towel to wring out the excess water
	* Fluff up the "puck" using a fork
## Snack
### Popcorn
* 100g serving of air-popped popcorn
* Graze throughout the day

## Dinner
### Protein
* Chicken
* Beef
* Eggs
### Vegetables
* Broccoli 
* Carrot
* Sweet Potato
* Asparagus
* Blanched Green Beans
* UV-Treated Mushrooms
### Additional
* Extra Virgin Olive Oil (small amount)
* Lemon Juice
* Ginger
